## The problem

Every week, leaders and their teams burn hours turning numbers into something presentable — copying data into spreadsheets, building charts one at a time, hand-writing the "so what," and fighting with formatting the night before the meeting. The insight is in the data; getting it *out* is the work.

## The solution

Paste your data. VIS reads it, understands what each column means, chooses the visuals that tell the story best, computes the headline metrics, writes the executive summary, flags the risks, and lays it all out as a polished, presentation-ready dashboard.

> **It's like having an analyst and a designer on call — instantly, for every dataset.**

## Everything you can do

- **💬 AI Assistant** — just ask: *"make it look like Apple," "reduce the charts," "build a presentation," "summarize the quarter."*

## Enterprise-ready

- **Your AI, your control.** Connect your own model. The API key is managed centrally by an admin and kept on the server — never shipped to end-user browsers.
- **Team administration.** A dedicated admin portal to manage credentials, set team-wide branding and defaults, and control access.
- **Deploys where you need it.** Runs in the cloud, on your own infrastructure, or fully **offline / air-gapped** for locked-down networks.
- **Zero heavy footprint.** Lightweight, fast, and dependency-light — quick to stand up, easy to run.

## Security & privacy

Your pasted data is analysed **in the browser**. Nothing is sent anywhere unless AI enrichment is enabled — and then only to the AI endpoint **you** configure, through a secure server-side proxy. No third-party analytics. No data resale. Your numbers stay your numbers.
